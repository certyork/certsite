Types of projects we engage in

Since we started in 2017, we have been involved in a wide variety of projects:

1) An evaluation of the Converge Theatre course in Northumbria University in Newcastle using interviews.

2) A project exploring what ‘Converge’ means to Converge students using photographs to support interviews.

3) An evaluation of an arts and mental health project in London using art to support interviews and a questionnaire in both 2019 and 2020.

4) An evaluation of a woman’s refuge, using interviews.

5) An evaluation of the York Multiple and Complex Needs Network, using interviews, and creating a theatre performance capturing our findings.

6) An evaluation of a project at Tang Hall Smart where we used music to support interviews.

7) An evaluation of St Nicks Ecotherapy project.

8) We are currently working on a project exploring mental health waiting rooms. {{ content | safe }}
